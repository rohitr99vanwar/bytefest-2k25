ByteFest 2025 - Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We’ve got answers.

1. What is ByteFest?
ByteFest is our annual college tech festival featuring competitions, workshops, and guest lectures focused on technology and innovation.
2. Who can participate?
ByteFest is open to all students of MBES College as well as participants from other colleges and universities.
3. How do I register for events?
You can register online through the official ByteFest website or at the registration booth during the fest days.
4. Are there any fees to participate?
Most events at ByteFest are free, but some workshops and special sessions may have a nominal registration fee.
5. What kind of events are there?
Events include hackathons, coding contests, robotics challenges, quiz competitions, tech talks, and workshops on emerging technologies.
6. Can I form a team for competitions?
Yes! Most team events allow groups of 2-4 participants. Check each event’s details for specific team size limits.
7. Will there be certificates and prizes?
Absolutely! Winners receive prizes and all participants get participation certificates.
8. Who do I contact for more info?
You can contact the ByteFest organizing committee via email at bytefest007@gmail.com
9. Is food provided during the fest?
Basic refreshments will be available on campus, but participants are encouraged to arrange their own meals.
10. Can I volunteer to help organize?
Yes! Volunteers are welcome. Please sign up through the volunteer section on the ByteFest website or contact the committee directly.
